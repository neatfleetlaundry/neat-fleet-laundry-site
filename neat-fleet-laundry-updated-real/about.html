About Neat Fleet Laundry

Neat Fleet Laundry is a local, owner‑operated pickup and delivery service built to make laundry day easier for families, professionals, and students across Wilmington, Newark, Middletown, Camden, and Dover.

Route‑based service

We follow a consistent north‑to‑south route every Friday — starting in New Castle County and ending in Kent County — so we can keep pricing fair and predictable for everyone.

Quality & care

Your clothes are sorted, washed, dried, and folded like they were our own. We offer fragrance‑free and hypoallergenic options and handle bedding, linens, and everyday laundry.

Local support

Have a question or special request? You’ll talk to someone local who understands the area and the route — not a call center.
