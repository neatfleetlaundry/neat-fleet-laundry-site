Frequently asked questions

When do you pick up and deliver?

We collect on Fridays along our north‑to‑south route and typically deliver back on Saturdays. Your exact window depends on your city.

What if I miss my pickup?

If we miss you, we’ll text you to reschedule the next available route. You’re never charged if we don’t collect your laundry.

Do Wilmington & Dover pay different prices?

No. Pricing is the same for all cities on our route — Wilmington through Dover — as long as you’re within our posted service area.

What detergents do you use?

We use high‑quality, commercial detergents with fragrance‑free and hypoallergenic options. Tell us your preferences in the notes.

How should I bag my laundry?

Place items in a sturdy bag or hamper you don’t mind us taking. We’ll return everything neatly folded in clean bags.
