Frequently asked questions

Which areas do you serve?

We currently serve New Castle County (Wilmington‑area) and Kent County (Dover‑area) along our Friday north‑to‑south route.

What days do you pick up and deliver?

Pickups are on Friday, and deliveries are typically on Saturday. Your exact pickup window depends on your city; you’ll see the options when you book.

Do Wilmington and Dover customers pay the same price?

Yes. Our wash & fold price is the same across our route. We don’t add extra delivery fees just because you’re further south.

Can I leave my laundry outside?

Yes. Many customers leave their bag on a porch or in a designated spot. Just let us know in the notes so our driver knows where to look.

What should I put my laundry in?

You can use a hamper, reusable bag, or sturdy trash bag. We’ll return your laundry neatly folded and bagged.

Do you offer subscriptions?

Yes. Weekly or bi‑weekly pickups are available and are a great fit for busy households or shared apartments. Mention that you’re interested in a subscription in your booking notes.

What detergents do you use?

We use quality detergents with fragrance‑free and hypoallergenic options. If you have sensitivities, list them in your notes so we can select the best option for you.

How do I get started?

Just visit the home page and click “Book Pickup”. Fill out the form with your address, city, and preferred window, and we’ll confirm by email or text.
