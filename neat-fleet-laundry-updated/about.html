About Neat Fleet Laundry

Neat Fleet Laundry is a local pickup and delivery laundry service serving New Castle and Kent County, from North Wilmington down through Dover.

We’re built around a simple idea: make laundry day disappear for busy households and professionals. Instead of spending hours sorting, washing, drying, and folding, you can leave a bag by the door and get everything back neatly folded and ready to put away.

Local & route-based

We follow a consistent north‑to‑south route every Friday, starting in North Wilmington and ending in Dover. That keeps our pricing fair whether you’re closer to Wilmington or closer to Dover.

Fast turnaround

Most orders are picked up on Friday and delivered on Saturday, so you’re never without your essentials for long. Rush options may be available depending on the week.

Care for your clothes

We treat your laundry as if it were our own. We offer fragrance‑free and hypoallergenic options and pay attention to special instructions noted in your booking.
